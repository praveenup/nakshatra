Instructions
  • To claim prizes, your college must be registered at the official site of Nakshatra.
  • Each college can have multiple submissions. But only one photo per person.
  • If you can't find your college in the list, Contact us at info@nakshatra19.com.
  • After registration you will randomly get a word that will be the theme for the photo that they take (word will be sent via E-mail, do check the spam folder).
  • The maximum size of the photo to be uploaded should be 5MB.
  • The photo must be either in jpeg, jpg or png format.
  • Participants must submit only original work with filename as collegeName_participantName. (eg. NITCalicut_xyz).
  • An unedited photograph has to be submitted along with its meaningful description based on the word selected.
  • Photo should not include trademarks, contract rights, or any other intellectual property rights of any other third person or entity.
  • Photo should not contain names, likenesses, or other characteristics identifying celebrities or other public figures, living or dead.
  • The judges will determine the winner based on two criteria,
    • The number of likes that the photo gets on the official Facebook page of Nakshatra.
    • The adherence of the photo and caption to the theme chosen by the participant.
  • The upload link will be active till the 23:59, 22nd of February, 2019.
  • Download PDF of rules here
  • Login here with your registered email and password to submit your photograph.
