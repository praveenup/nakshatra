About

Nakshatra

Nakshatra is an all India MCA meet organised by the ambitious students of the MCA department. The two day technical fest is organised under the Computer Science and Engineering Department of NIT, Calicut. The responses received last few years were very encouraging and it has motivated us to take a step further and make Nakshatra'19 even bigger than last year's. This technical meet indulges an array of both technical as well as non-technical events along with talks by renowned and respected individuals from the IT industry. Hope to see you there!
